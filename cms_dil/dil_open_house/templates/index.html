With Projects and Research Through the DIL Network


Last year, with support from USAID, the Blum Center for Developing Economies founded the Development Impact Lab (DIL) in partnership with the Center for Effective Global Action (CEGA) in order to build a pipeline for novel technologies and approaches that aim to improve the lives of those who live on less than $2 a day.

Forming a global nexus for UC Berkeley’s mission of teaching, research and public service, DIL promotes technology design in the service of the world’s poor and provides training to a new generation of globally engaged scientists, engineers, and other interdisciplinary development practitioners across the university campus.
